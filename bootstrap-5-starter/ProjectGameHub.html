T1 WINS WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP

T1 and Lee “Faker” Sang-hyeok, the greatest organization and greatest player in League of Legends history, added to their legacy this weekend as they won the 2023 League of Legends World Championship. T1 and Faker lifted the Summoner’s Cup for the fourth time, but the first time since 2016 and for the first time in front of their home fans in South Korea at Gocheok Sky Dome. The final brought in a record-setting peak audience of 6.4 million, shattering the previous League of Legends esports record of 5.1 million, which was set at last year’s Worlds final. In fact, the 6.4 million peak is the record for any esports event, per Esports Charts.
